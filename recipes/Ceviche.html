Ceviche

Ceviche, just perfection!

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of fresh fish (white fish! also, not really, every kind of fish but with the correct texture)
  • 12 fresh lemons
  • 1 onion (purple, purple onion)
  • 1 minced chili pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of ground hot pepper
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 coriander branch
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger juice
  • Salt and pepper

Extras

    1 cup of cancha serrana 2 shelled parboiled corn 2 sweet potatoes, parboiled and sliced 4 leaves of fresh lettuce 200 grams of warm squid pork rinds

How to make Ceviche? Steps!

  1. Wash and chop the fish into cubes, then put it in a bowl and add salt, garlic, hot pepper, ginger and a pinch of pepper.
  2. Chop the chili pepper into slices, previously remove the veins and seeds. Also cut the celery and coriander very finely, add these ingredients to the bowl and mix.
  3. Squeeze the lemons and add the juice to the bowl, stir well and let sit for 5 minutes.
  4. To finish, add the onion and mix everything evenly, adjust the salt level. Serve immediately.
